Daily Tours

Take a walk and discover the stories of the Blue Mansion

What is it like to go back in time, to live an illustrious life in the grandeur of 19th-century Penang? As you immerse yourself in the history of the Blue Mansion, our trained guides will provide detailed and comprehensive insight into the heritage and architecture of the mansion. You will also get a peek into the lavish lifestyle of Cheong Fatt Tze.

A Must See if you are visiting George Town.

Daily Tour Times and Entrance Fees are listed below

Tours are available DAILY at 11:00am, 2:00pm and 3.30pm Duration: 45 minutes.

RM17.00 per adult (including GST) RM8.50 per child under 12 years (including GST)

Tours are conducted in English. We also offer tours in Mandarin, Japanese and other languages with prior arrangement and Special tours can also be organized for private groups and organizations. Please contact the concierge or email us at reservations@cheongfatttzemansion.com for more information.
