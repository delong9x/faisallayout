Courtyard [Patio]
For Cheong Fatt Tze, the most important meal of the day is breakfast.
To him, it simply meant sustenance, rejuvenation and longevity. Here at The Courtyard [Patio], the elements of ‘feng’ (wind) meets ‘shui’ (water), creating an elegant space in the Mansion with the strongest natural energy flow.
As you savour your breakfast under the open sky and amid the morning mist, choose from a variety of delectable dishes, from nutella crepes to nasi lemak and set the day right.
